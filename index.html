The Problem Space

Why Pakistan Needs a Blockchain Election

⚠

Ballot Tampering

Paper ballots are physically altered, destroyed, or replaced. Rigging at polling stations is historically documented.

→
Immutable blockchain records — every vote cryptographically sealed.
👤

Ghost Voters

Fake CNIC entries allow individuals to vote multiple times under different identities.

→
CNIC hashed on-chain. One CNIC = One vote. Forever.
🔒

Lack of Transparency

Counting happens behind closed doors. Results are announced without verifiable audit trails.

→
Real-time, public vote count on Ethereum. Anyone can verify.
📋

Candidate Vetting

Unqualified or disqualified candidates participate due to weak verification (Article 62/63).

→
Smart contract enforces age, nationality & criminal record checks.
